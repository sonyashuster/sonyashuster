Data Collection

At Mapping Marvels, we have all the skills and tools to collect data to meet your needs. We have the ability to do field surveys using any of: total stations, spirit levels, and GPS units. We also have experience with creating surveys on ArcGIS's Survey123 system and using it as part of the data collection process - most recently to collect data about trees. In addition to field surveys, we also have experience working with satellite imagery and other remote sensing systems to extract data and understand a study area.

Once we have the raw data, we are able to use ETL (Extract Transform and Load) processes to bring the data into a database for easier management. Once the data is in a database, we are able to use our knowledge SQL to work with the data, find ways to make the data storage more efficient, and search the database for needed information.

Spatial Analysis

Once all the data has been collected, sorted, and checked for quality, the analysis can begin. Spatial data is a field that has really ballooned over the past 40 years. Although data collection is an important step, transferring that data into knowledge can sometimes be a challenge. If you want help with making sense of your spatial data come to us. We have spatial analysts who have been specially trained at Fleming College and can handle all your analysis needs.
